# 📌 Red Hat OpenShift AI (RHOAI) — Introduction

## 🎯 Objective

Understand what RHOAI is and why it is important in OpenShift environments.

---

## 🧠 What is RHOAI?

Red Hat OpenShift AI (RHOAI) is a platform that enables:

* Machine Learning workloads
* AI model training
* Data science pipelines

---

## 🏗️ Why Use RHOAI?

* Integrated with OpenShift
* Scalable workloads
* Enterprise-ready AI platform

---

## 🧩 Key Features

* Jupyter Notebooks
* Model Serving
* Pipelines
* GPU Support

---

## 📦 Use Cases

* Predictive analytics
* Data science projects
* AI model deployment

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

* Confusing RHOAI with plain Kubernetes AI tools
* Ignoring resource requirements

---

## 🔥 Exam Tip

👉 Focus on **installation + verification**, not deep AI concepts.
